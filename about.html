About

All the images are captured by Raquel Marie with a Canon Power Shot SX, an analog Minolta Xd 11, an Olympus OM-D, or an iPhone.

Inspired and influenced by street photography from the late 19th century to the early and mid 20th century, Raquel Marie’s personal style of photography is predominately black and white. She aims to take photographs that look timeless with more of a focus on natual light, shadows, and composition. Within her photographs, she aspires to take each viewer on a trip to the past, to a time when technology and artificial intelligence were still unimaginable.

Below are my favorite street photographers that inspire me and the style of photography that I aspire to emanate.

The Photographers
